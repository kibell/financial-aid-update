2024 Financial Aid Updates

Due to nationwide delays in federal financial aid processing this year, we’re currently experiencing high call volumes and limited appointment availability.

To speak with an SFS counselor, please email us at  studentfinance@snhu.edu  and we'll respond as quickly as we can. Alternatively, you can reach us directly at  877.455.7648 . If you encounter longer than usual hold times, please consider calling at another time or sending us an email.


Below are answers to many of the questions students are asking us this year about the 2024 financial aid delays.

The questions in the red box are optional. Upon logging in to your financial aid offer page, you will see a red box at the top of your screen with questions from the financial aid office. If you wish to answer these questions, you may do so by clicking the View Questions button. Skipping these questions does not affect your eligibility for financial aid or how SNHU will process disbursements to your account. Your Cost of Attendance estimate is not what you owe. When you click on the Offer tab, you will see information about your estimated Cost of Attendance (COA). The Department of Education requires SNHU to include estimates of both billable and non-billable charges in your COA. Billable charges are charges due directly to the university. Non-billable charges are an ESTIMATE of charges you MAY incur while attending school. You do not need to hit the SUBMIT button to receive your financial aid. There is a gray Submit button at the bottom of your financial aid offer page. SNHU students are not able to click this button. (No action is needed for this button.)
